The Schedule Guy

The schedule guy is a full-stack interactive web application that allows users to input personal events into a seamless and familiar calendar and it will notify you of those upcoming events via SMS. The application features JWT login-authentication and Twilio messaging support in the Back-End.

The idea for this project developed during the mist of COVID-19. I wanted to create a different way for parents to stay organized of their kids assignments during home-school and people usually don't pay much attention to the piles of notifications but they always look at text messages. This is where an idea became development.
